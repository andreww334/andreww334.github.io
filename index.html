FAQs

Will the wedding be indoors or outdoors?

The ceremony is outdoors on grass, followed by a patio cocktail hour and an indoor reception.

How do I get to Greenfield Farm?

If you choose to drive, please use the address 2421 Bagley Road, Cumming, GA 30041. We will have complimentary valet parking as you enter the property.

Ubers & Lyfts are also easily accessible to and from the venue if you prefer not to drive.

Can I bring a guest or my children?

We wish we could celebrate with everyone. However, to stay within our venue's strict capacity, we can only host the guests named on your invitation.

What time should I arrive?

The ceremony will promptly begin at 3:30 PM. Please arrive at least 15–30 minutes prior to the ceremony to enjoy the grounds and find your seat. Once the ceremony begins, we will be unable to seat additional guests.

What should I wear?

We kindly ask guests to wear formal attire. For men, this means a suit and tie; for women, a floor-length or mid-length dress. Please keep the outdoor setting in mind when choosing your footwear.
